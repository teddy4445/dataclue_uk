Our Selected Projects

We really love our job!

Secure Signature

We develop for a cybersecurity company an algorithm that allows saying if two signatures are from the same person or not. Our algorithm which is based on a recurrent neural network with an architecture specifically developed for this task can say with 98.2% accuracy if this is your signature after seeing only seven signatures. The client was an American company providing development services to the USA government

Optimal RL dual-parameter space search

We develop a specific optimization algorithm for the dual space of any parameter space of actions and policies given in an RL task for robotics in the discrete space. The client was an American based company with an Israeli office. The whole process was managed with the Israeli entity.

Multi-Language Search Engine

The client needed a search engine to operate both in Hebrew and English for a given database of possible results. Later, support for Russian has been added as a second project. The client was a Russian based company which was willing to enter the Israeli Ecom market.

Recommended Business in the City

An Israeli start-up company that provides a marketing service using its app wishes to provide a recommendation engine that tells users based on their history and current travel path what business nearby may be interesting for them.

Crops classifier and optimal coverage problem

We developed a crop classifier based on images taken by drones for start. Later, we develop an optimal coverage problem of a field with multiple drowns. The client was an Israeli start-up in the field of precis agriculture.

The best Clients for Italian Goods

The client’s main question was “which clients are the best buyers to each and every item in his Italian store?” We developed a customer segmentation model for the client’s international and demographic database of sales. The client was an Italian non-brand clothes distributor
