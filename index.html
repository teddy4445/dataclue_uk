ESG & Climate Solution

Investor sentiments and regulators

As investors become ever more conscious of their ESG & Climate footprint… their sentiments shift towards greener, more responsible investments. It can no longer be, just about the amount of return but HOW that return is generated. With increasing regulatory pressure, mandating firms to disclose their ESG & Climate standings and more scrutiny on how green their investments are. Financial institutions and corporates need to implement a transparent & robust ESG & Climate framework to aid their investment & strategic decision making.

Problems with Data

ESG & Climate data is often inconsistent; it’s difficult to separate ESG & Climate news from ESG & Climate related noise…

Support functions have limited capabilities to comply with the evolving requirements…

ESG & Climate Scoring methodologies and underlying reference data from third party providers are often greenwashed, opaque and disparate…

Analytics platform Solution

Which is why DataClue have developed an intelligent ESG & Climate Analytics Platform, a transparent, objective and customizable tool that helps our clients - Harness the right ESG & Climate data; be in control of their ESG & Climate methodology; and make confident investment decisions.

Orama

ESG & Climate Data

Orama allows clients to select and pool structured ESG & Climate reference data from several providers & unstructured data from multiple sources. Unstructured data sources include: News articles, social media posts, blogs, NGO reports, research reports, web pages et. al… Our platform can even pull out ESG & Climate data from dark data pools such as legal documents, trade documents, other sources using Natural language Processing techniques Thus allowing clients to define and customize their own ESG & Climate data universe.

ESG & Climate Methodology

Orama provides clients a transparent and customizable glass-box ESG & Climate methodology and a bespoke ESG & Climate score unlike other providers.

Clients can drill down into their scores to investigate what's driving them. Therefore, providing a deeper insight into the root-cause analysis.

They have the flexibility to tailor ESG & Climate data and statistical models to analyse underlying ESG & Climate data & market sentiments.

Simultaneously analysing both structured and unstructured data, applying machine learning and AI models, our clients can decipher advanced signals to identify the risk of greenwashing.

ESG & Climate Solution

Once configured DataClue Orama© will provide clients:

Flexibility to customise ESG & Climate analysis and supporting data universe to meet intended objective such as alpha generation, investment decision making, risk management or regulatory compliance.

A transparent bespoke score that is explainable to investors (and board), defendable to regulators and facilitates portfolio selection process.

Analyse advanced signals to identify greenwashing risk through monitoring of news articles, blogs, NGO reports. Calculate ESG & Climate risk exposure for required for stress testing and scenario analysis and other regulatory requirements.

Portfolio analytics & investment decision making tool that can be integrated with and refreshed on a real time basis (where data is available).

Report generating and dynamic dashboarding capability utilising portfolio or trade book positions and constituent knowledge.
